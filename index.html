What This Is

Hello and welcome to the Gat-AR tour, This is a fun way for Students or vistors to explore the campus and see what UF has to offer. There are a lot of landmarks around campus see if you can find them all!

In Person Tour

If you find yourself on our beautiful campus, take part in this AR scavenger hunt powered by echoAR. Scan the different landmarks to recieve facts about each one!

Online Tour

Participate from the comfort of your home! UF offers the luxury of complete your degree online, so we decided to do the same with our scavenger hunt! Scan the photos provided on our website to get facts of each of the places and feel like you are on campus!
